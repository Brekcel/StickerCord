To use this bot:

Register with your Discord account via the commands below.
Once registered, send the sticker you wish to put to this bot.
Then type ".s" (without the quotes) on the server you wish to send the sticker to.
Your message will then be deleted and replaced with the sticker.

Commands:

*/help* Displays the help message. (This)

*/register DISCORD_NAME#ID* Begins registration of your Discord with your Telegram account

*/unregister* Removes your Discord accounts association with your Telegram account
